I create systems people want to use, not just tolerate.

I’m Olga, a Product Designer based in Amsterdam.

Over the past decade, I’ve turned complex, frustrating systems into intuitive products that people enjoy using — bringing calm to busy environments and helping them work with confidence.

My work

From POS systems and e-commerce platforms to logistics dashboards and enterprise tools, I design for high-stakes environments where complexity meets real people.

I’ve built products that improved training speeds by 40%, reduced operational errors by over 70%, and increased sales by double digits. I’m proud that many are still in use years later, quietly making everyday work simpler for teams around the world.
